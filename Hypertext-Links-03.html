    #3: Smash Bros Ultimate (switch edition)

    Super Smash Bros: Ultimate (switch edition) was released on december 7th, 2018 by Nintendo. It's a very fun to play and multiplayer compatible pvp style game, with several combat modes that fit to players' preferences. It features 89 characters in total, most are from nintendo games but there are a few others Inculded such as Banjo & Kazooie and Minecraft's Steve. For people who are still learning how to play and aren't ready to compete against other players, there is a story mode avaliable and you can fight against low or high level cpus depending on your skill level, respectively. The Online interface it has is great as well, you can participate in tournaments of up to 32 people or do single battles with people all over the world.
